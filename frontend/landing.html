Everything you need

A complete knowledge management system, right on your server.

Markdown Editor

Full GFM support with live preview, split view, formatting toolbar, and keyboard shortcuts.

Syntax Highlighting

30+ languages with line numbers via highlight.js. Themes auto-adapt to your color scheme.

Wikilinks & Graph

Link notes with [[wikilinks]]. Visualize your knowledge graph interactively.

Mermaid Diagrams

Flowcharts, sequence diagrams, gantt charts, and more — rendered right inside your notes.

MathJax

Beautiful LaTeX math equations inline and display mode. From simple formulas to complex notation.

16+ Themes

Dracula, Nord, Catppuccin, One Dark, Rosé Pine, Solarized, GitHub, and more. Dark & light.

Multi-User & OIDC

Built-in auth or connect your identity provider — Authelia, Keycloak, and any OIDC-compatible service.

Note Sharing

Share individual notes via public links with unique tokens. One click to create or revoke access.

Mobile Ready

Responsive design with bottom navigation, touch-friendly controls, and optimized layouts for any screen.
